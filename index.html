Pandan Cake

Get introduced with a unique flavour named Pandan flavour. Pandan is actually a Thai leaf which has an unique sweet and light aroma. Traditionally,desiccated coconut crumbs are used in Pandan flavoured Cake.Hope you’ll love to taste the new and unique flavour. Don't underestimate the flavour by thinking it to be 'betel leaf' cake.
